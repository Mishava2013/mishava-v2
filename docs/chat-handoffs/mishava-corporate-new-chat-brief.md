## Short Current Status

Mishava Corporate / Institutional is a future private institutional surface. It is not a live product.

Current Corporate state:

- `corporate.mishava.org` is supported by subdomain routing.

- No vendor approval/certification claims.
- No procurement-safe claims.

- No AI final trust outcomes.
- No internal/private notes exposed publicly.
