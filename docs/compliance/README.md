# Mishava NGO Compliance Readiness

Status: internal readiness documentation. This folder does not claim certification, authorization, legal approval, or external audit completion.

## Purpose

This folder tracks the compliance, accessibility, privacy, security, and future audit evidence work needed before Mishava NGO claims institutional readiness for future compliance reviews.

Mishava is preparing for future reviews. Mishava is not SOC 2 certified. Mishava is not ISO 27001 certified. Mishava is not FedRAMP authorized. Mishava has not completed a formal VPAT/ACR. Accessibility work targets WCAG 2.2 AA where feasible, but it has not been externally certified.

## Documents

- `soc-2-readiness.md` maps SOC 2 readiness areas, current foundations, planned controls, and evidence needed.
- `iso-27001-readiness.md` maps ISO 27001 readiness areas and policy gaps.
- `accessibility-vpat-readiness.md` tracks WCAG, Section 508, and VPAT/ACR readiness.
- `privacy-data-governance.md` inventories data categories and governance gaps.
- `security-control-gaps.md` tracks known security and operational control gaps.
- `audit-evidence-index.md` plans future evidence folders and artifacts.

## Approved Posture Language

Use conservative language:

- preparing for;
- designed with;
- readiness work;
- targeting WCAG 2.2 AA;
- not yet externally audited;
- not yet certified;
- draft baseline;
- subject to attorney or specialist review.

Avoid implying completion of a formal certification, authorization, audit, VPAT/ACR, or legal review until that process is complete.

## Current Readiness Boundaries

- Compliance readiness is documentation and control preparation only.
- External audits and certifications are future work.
- Formal legal approval is future work.
- Production monitoring, backup/restore drill, penetration test, and formal access review remain gaps.
- Payment cannot affect trust outcomes.
- AI remains suggestion-only and human-reviewed.
- Raw evidence files remain private by default.
